PRIVATE COMPANY CORPORATE GOVERNANCE COMPLIANCE REPORT GENERATOR

This document provides summary of the general compliance obligations statutorily imposed on Private Companies in Nigeria under the Federal laws (excluding industry specific regulations, internal policies/codes and state/local government laws) .

It is expected that a compliance audit be conducted at the end of each financial year. On completion of the audit exercise which will be undertaken by the Company’s Internal Compliance Team ; a compliance report is expected to be submitted to the Board.

This report generator makes provision for the inclusion of other sector specific/internal rules to which the Company is subject and a provision for explanations where the Company is not fully compliant (factors that have hindered the Company’s full compliance within the period under review including exposures and other liabilities which may arise on account of any failure to comply) in accordance with the “Apply and Explain” principle under the Nigerian Code of Corporate Governance, 2018
